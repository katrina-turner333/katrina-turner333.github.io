Reflection

The three new things I have learned about myself and my ego as a result of the core learning:
1. I learned about taking regular breaks and how important they are when doing difficult problem solving. It helps keep me calm.
2. My ego feels damaged when the code doesn’t work and I feel frustrated if I don’t understand after spending some time on the problem.
3. I realised I need to work more on my emotional intelligence and my patience.

What surprised me the most about the core learning: Rubber ducky concept

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?
Finding out that I need to work on a few areas such as patience and emotional intelligence.

I think Dev Academy, a programming school, spend so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course because:
The skills this teaches you can help in your career as a programmer and can set the Dev Academy candidates apart from other candidates when searching for work.
